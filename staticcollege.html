Vision

To impart quality technical education through research, innovation, and teamwork for creating professionally superior and ethically strong manpower that meets global challenges.

Mission

  • Modernize education using ICT-enabled teaching methodologies.
  • Prepare students for entrepreneurship, employability, and research.
  • Instill ethics and life-long learning values in students.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  • Apply knowledge in designing computer-based systems addressing industrial and social needs.
  • Develop software solutions using modern tools and techniques.

Courses Offered

  • B.Tech in Computer Engineering
  • M.Tech in Computer Engineering
  • Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering
